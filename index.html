Who Am I?

Well Hello, my name Is Charles Bateson and some of you may well know me as just another Bartender, quenching the thirst of many Sydney musicians, but what you may not know is im also a qualified Personal Trainer and Massage Therapist and have worked for companies such as The Sheraton Hotel, Vision Personal Training Studios & Premier Health and Fitness. I have juggled the mixed lifestyle of health and fitness and the dark corners of late night entertainment and can see how the cross over for many can be too daunting or not viable in the conventional sense. And thats what im here to help with. To dedicate what i have learnt and offer the many musicians who have given their lives to pleasing the masses a chance to give back to themselves in one simple class.

What To Expect

A fun and easy way to exercise without having to change your life. The Classes are Time base so you go at your own pace, The Class instructors, encourage and motivate you to make sure everyone is benefiting and getting the most out of the class. They personally demonstrate and assist you in doing the exercises correctly.

What To Bring

A towel or yoga mat to do exercises on. Relevant clothing. A sweat rag, and a big cheesy smile, knowing every class is an active step to a healthier body
